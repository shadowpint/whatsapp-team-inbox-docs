## Overview

Intelligent conversation assignment ensures customers reach the right team member quickly through automated routing, skill-based matching, and load balancing.

See [Admin: Conversation Assignment](/admin/conversation-assignment) for configuration details.

## Assignment Methods

- **Round Robin**: Even distribution
- **Load-Based**: Assign to least busy agent
- **Skill-Based**: Match expertise with issue type
- **Manual**: Team members self-assign
