## WhatsApp Conversation Pricing

WhatsApp charges per conversation (24-hour window):

### By Region

| Region | User-Initiated | Business-Initiated |
|--------|---------------|-------------------|
| North America | $0.0085/conv | $0.0160/conv |
| Western Europe | $0.0158/conv | $0.0298/conv |
| India | $0.0040/conv | $0.0110/conv |
| Latin America | $0.0095/conv | $0.0300/conv |
| Rest of World | Varies | Varies |

**Example calculation (North America):**
- 1,000 user-initiated conversations Ã— $0.0085 = $8.50
- 500 business-initiated conversations Ã— $0.0160 = $8.00
- **Total: $16.50/month**

### Cost Optimization

**Free conversations:**
- Respond within 24 hours to user-initiated messages
- Each 24-hour window is FREE if customer messaged first

**Paid conversations:**
- Business-initiated messages (you contact first)
- Template messages after 24-hour window
