## Before Contacting Support

Help us help you faster:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check Documentation">
    Search docs for your question - most issues are covered
  </Step>

  <Step title="Search GitHub Issues">
    Your question may already be answered
  </Step>

  <Step title="Gather Information">
    When reporting issues, include:
    - Error messages (full text)
    - Steps to reproduce
    - Environment (OS, Node version, hosting)
    - Logs (if applicable)
  </Step>
</Steps>
