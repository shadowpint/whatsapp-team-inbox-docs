## General Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What is WhatsApp Team Inbox?">
    WhatsApp Team Inbox is an open-source, self-hosted solution for managing WhatsApp Business conversations with your team. It's a DIY alternative to expensive SaaS products like Intercom or Zendesk.

    **Key benefits:**
    - Own your data
    - Lower monthly costs ($20-150/month vs $300+/month)
    - Full customization
    - No vendor lock-in
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How is this different from WhatsApp Business App?">
    **WhatsApp Business App:**
    - Single user only
    - Mobile app
    - Free
    - Limited features

    **WhatsApp Team Inbox:**
    - Multiple team members
    - Web-based
    - Self-hosted (infrastructure costs)
    - Advanced features (assignment, analytics, integrations)
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do I need technical knowledge to set this up?">
    **Basic setup:** 1-2 hours with our guides (no coding required)

    **Requirements:**
    - Ability to follow step-by-step instructions
    - Access to cloud hosting (Railway, Render, AWS)
    - WhatsApp Business API account

    **Optional:** Developer for custom features
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How much does it cost?">
    **Small team (500 conversations/month):** $20-30/month
    **Medium team (2,000 conversations):** $120-150/month
    **Large team (10,000 conversations):** $450-500/month

    **Costs include:**
    - Server hosting
    - Database
    - Email service
    - WhatsApp conversation fees

    [Detailed cost breakdown →](/cost/monthly-costs)
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I migrate from Intercom/Zendesk?">
    Yes! We provide migration guides for:
    - Exporting customer data
    - Importing to Team Inbox
    - Switching WhatsApp integration

    [Migration guide →](/support/migration-guide)
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## WhatsApp-Specific Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Do I need WhatsApp Business API?">
    Yes. The standard WhatsApp Business **App** only supports a single user. You need WhatsApp Business **API** for multi-agent access.

    **How to get API access:**
    1. Create Meta Business Account
    2. Apply for WhatsApp Business API
    3. Get business verified
    4. Register phone number

    [WhatsApp setup guide →](/deployment/whatsapp-setup)
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How much does WhatsApp charge?">
    WhatsApp charges per conversation (not per message):

    **User-initiated:** $0.0085-0.0158 per conversation
    **Business-initiated:** $0.0110-0.0300 per conversation

    **1 conversation = 24-hour window**

    **Free conversations:**
    - When customer messages you first
    - If you respond within 24 hours

    [WhatsApp pricing details →](/cost/monthly-costs)
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can customers message me first?">
    Yes! Customers can initiate conversations by:
    - Scanning QR code
    - Clicking wa.me link
    - Messaging your WhatsApp number directly
    - From Facebook/Instagram (with integration)

    Customer-initiated messages are FREE for 24 hours!
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What are message templates?">
    Templates are pre-approved messages for business-initiated conversations. Required when:
    - You message customer first
    - >24 hours since customer's last message

    **Examples:**
    - Order confirmations
    - Shipping updates
    - Appointment reminders

    Approval takes 1-24 hours.

    [Template guide →](/deployment/whatsapp-setup)
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Are there message limits?">
    **Messaging tier system:**
    - Tier 1: 1,000 business-initiated conversations/day
    - Tier 2: 10,000/day (auto-upgrade with good quality)
    - Tier 3: 100,000/day
    - Tier 4: Unlimited

    **Quality matters:**
    - Maintain &gt;50% reply rate
    - Keep spam reports &lt;1%
    - High quality = higher limits
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Pricing & Billing

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Is there a free tier?">
    The software is free and open-source, but you pay for:
    - Infrastructure (servers, database)
    - WhatsApp conversation fees
    - Optional services (email, monitoring)

    **Free tiers available:**
    - Database: Supabase (500 MB)
    - Email: SendGrid (100/day)
    - Monitoring: Uptime Robot (50 monitors)

    **Estimated minimum:** $15-25/month for small team
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I track costs?">
    **Built-in cost tracking:**
    - WhatsApp conversation counter
    - Infrastructure dashboard
    - Cost projection tools

    **External tools:**
    - AWS Cost Explorer
    - Cloud provider billing dashboards
    - Custom analytics

    [Cost monitoring →](/admin/monitoring)
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I reduce costs?">
    Yes! Optimization tips:
    - Respond within 24h (avoid paid messages)
    - Right-size infrastructure
    - Use free tier services
    - Archive old data
    - Optimize database queries

    **Typical savings: 30-60%**

    [Optimization guide →](/cost/optimization-tips)
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
